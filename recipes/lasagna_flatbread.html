Lasagna Flatbread

Lasagna Flatbread is a cheesy and crispy Continental appetizer which is loaded with three types of delectable cheese and chicken sausage. This appetizer recipe has an exciting yet overwhelming taste that will be lingering in your mouth for a while.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 (3 ounce) package Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 pound sausage
  • Â½ (26 ounce) jar marinara sauce
  • 6 flatbreads

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Combine ricotta cheese, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese,
    Parmesan cheese, egg, and Italian seasoning in a bowl.
  3. Cook sausage in a skillet over medium heat until no longer pink,
    to 10 minutes; drain. Stir in marinara sauce.
  4. Spread 1/6 of the cheese mixture evenly on each flatbread; cover with
    sausage mixture. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, 10 to 15
    minutes.
